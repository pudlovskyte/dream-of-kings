Play Areas

There will be a few areas designated as roleplay-only. Strive to keep everything in character at all times, and leave these areas for any Out-of-Game activity.

Such areas will be revealed before the game starts, separatelly for each Sect, during the introductory meetings.

All combats should be handled outside these areas.

Otherwise, the official play area is the whole city of Vilnius, during the nighttime in regural weekdays and any time during both weekends, regardless of luminocity.
