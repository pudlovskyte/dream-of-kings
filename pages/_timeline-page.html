Prologue

Play by Email and Introductory Meetings

We will arrange separate meetings for most of the Camarilla and Sabbat to make introductions, answer questions, and provide last second information about the city. During this period, declarations and play by email will be allowed, and players can engage in limited in‐character interaction in their own groups. Establishing additional backstory with other players will be encouraged, but meetings with other players will be considered as outside the game (or having taken place in the past).

Sep 24 – Oct 02
{% include 'elements/_calendar.html' %}

Main Game

This period includes two full weekends and a week between them. Once the arrivals come to Vilnius the game begins in earnest.

In the main game all bets are off, and any character is in danger of a Final Death.

October 02

Epilogue

At the end of our story we will round up all the events that happened and conclude the game.
