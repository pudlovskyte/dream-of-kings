Rules

The rules will be simplified, adjusted for a shorter term game. The combat will prove deadlier and quicker, the intrigue will be simple and slighty choreographed in advance.

Combat

In some ways, the system is a step back, and deliberately lacks rules complex actions and long series of coin tosses.

Current rules status: Playtesting.

Influence and Intrigue

A certain positions of strength will be given out for the local kindred, same as arrivals will achieve certain assets based on the extent of their influence focus. There will be events and struggles for influence over certain areas that will require a number of characters to traverse the city.

Current rules status: Conceptual.
