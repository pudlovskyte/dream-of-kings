Incentives for Groups

Players are encouraged to form groups focusing on a particular common goal. For Camarilla, the groups should be clan‐based, whereas Sabbat should focus on forming packs. Every group will be contacted detailing their goals. A dialogue will follow in order to integrate player needs and wishes into the upcoming plot.

Every player starts as a Neonate. Every three players can nominate a fourth player for Ancillae position. This nomination is player based, and it is encouraged to choose more experienced players for such roles, even across groups.

The Game Masters will assign a couple Elders from the ranks of Ancillae. Elders will have less freedom for their backstory and goals, as well as other play limitations. It is permitted to decline the “promotion”.

Currently, a 12 player group allows for 9 Neonates, 2 Ancillae and 1 Elder, but this is subject to change.
